My Story - Aleksandra Henschke - Teaching is Fun

They say: when your work is your passion, it will give you wings.

My life passion for providing assistance with English created My Company.

Aleksandra Henschke - Teaching is Fun.

Sharing my knowledge is joyful; this is why

Teaching is Fun!

I had an amazing opportunity to fall crazy in love with languages, as my parents taught me that when you learn, you are free.

It became obvious to me as a 3-year-old baby that English = LOVE.

I knew in High School that I wanted to be a teacher; however, life has its twists and turns, and I needed to wait for the right circumstances. But it did not stop me during my studies at Warsaw School of Economics from HELPING others with English - such a 5-year barter; I needed support in maths ;).

Work, work, work for International Corporations in Poland, India, The US, and Portugal required constant English education - I could help others with English at my jobs.

For years I was connected with Customer Services, and I was providing my friends with help in English, and TODAY...

Aleksandra Henschke - Teaching is Fun combines all my passions:

Sharing knowledge, Language Coaching, and Customer Services.

It's been 20 years already - No matter where I worked, I always cared for my English education. This is why I signed up for courses such as Customer Services, Law, Advertisement, or Business English.

Currently, as a tutor, I focus on improving my skills. In order to stay up to date with the language, I take part in English courses for tutors.

I care for QUALITY; I deliver quantity with quality.

English is so much more than sharing my knowledge; it is essential to connect with my Clients - supporting them in achieving their business goals, finding their strengths, and providing mental support! Remember - You can achieve any goal you want!
